🎯 MARKET PRICE WORKSHEET (Terapeak)
Research your slow movers in Seller Hub → Research → Terapeak. Type the median SOLD price below. The app tells you instantly if dropping to market is still a profit or a real loss.
📖 How to read Terapeak — tap to open
1. Search your item by name or OEM part number.
2. Look at "Avg sold price" — that's your starting number.
3. Sanity check it. Scroll to the sold listings below. Find the 3-4 that actually match YOUR item's condition. If junk/stripped units are dragging the average down (e.g. $45 lows pulling down a $211 reality), use the realistic comparable price — not the raw average.
4. Type that realistic median into the box below. The app does the rest — verdict + target price.
5. Also check sell-through % — high (70%+) means it WILL sell; low means saturated, price aggressively.
● PROFIT market price clears your floor — drop it, no loss   ● THIN tiny margin — drop only to move it   ● LOSS underwater — decide: sell old stock or bundle
